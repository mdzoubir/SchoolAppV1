English courses for pre-teens ages 9 to 12

The pre-teens learn English through their curiosity

At this age, the children are at a stage of self-discovery. In the Animal Planet and Fairy Tales courses, we encourage them to develop their creative and communication skills so they can express their opinion in English in familiar situations.

English for children aged 9

Animal Planet 1

Course objectives: in these classes, the 9-year-old children learn vocabulary related to nature, animals, and taking care of the planet, by joining Alexandra on her adventures. The readings are more extense and they perfect their oral communication with short dialogues in English which are useful for day-to-day life.

Class length:60 minutes, twice a week.

Small groups:each class has a maximum of 8 students.

At home: the topics covered in class address the challenges and dreams of the pre-teens’ day-to-day lives, which is a reason to put them into practise in a real-life situation, like whilst at home.

English for children aged 10

Animal Planet 2

Course objectives: For the second part of Animal Planet, we revise different themes to expand their English vocabulary and grammatical structures. The children learn how to buy or order food in a restaurant, and they can also explain how their holidays went or give recommendations about how to fight climate change.

Class length: 60 minutes, twice a week.

Small groups: each class has a maximum of 8 students.

At home: the topics covered in class address the challenges and dreams of the pre-teens’ day-to-day lives, which is a reason to put them into practise in a real-life situation, like whilst at home.

English for children aged 11

Fairy Tales 1

Course objectives: In this course, we adapt traditional fairy tales to a more contemporary setting. For example, in Red Riding Hoody, Little Red Riding Hood is now a champion BMX rider and in Sidney and his Lucky Scarf, Cinderella is a boy who moves in with his family abroad and they take advantage of the fact he doesn’t know much English.

Class lenght: Ninety minutes, twice a week.

Small groups: each class has a maximum of 8 students.

At home: the topics covered in class address the challenges and dreams of the pre-teens’ day-to-day lives, which is a reason to put them into practise in a real-life situation, like whilst at home.

English for children aged 12

Fairy Tales 2

Class length: 90 minutes, twice a week.

Small groups: each class has a maximum of 8 students.

At home: the topics covered in class address the challenges and dreams of the pre-teens’ day-to-day lives, which is a reason to put them into practise in a real-life situation, like whilst at home.

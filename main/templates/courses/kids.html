The children learn English without realising

By recreating situations they are familiar with, the children ages 3 to 8 learn English in a natural way. The stories of Sam, Emma, Oliver, Marcia, Pam&Paul, and Ben&Brenda spark their imagination and curiosity to continue learning the language.

Science workshop for children

What is the Mad Scientist?

It is important that the children do the science workshop in English accompanied by a family member. The aim is for adults to also learn interesting science facts together with their children and thus foster an appropriate environment for learning English as a family.

What does this workshop consist of?

Each Mad Scientist session focuses on a particular scientific phenomenon, such as reactions or density. During the workshop, the children observe, explore, sing, and learn all kinds of interesting things in English.

Ages: aimed at children ages 5 to 9.
Calendar: contact your nearest school to find out where the workshop is held and when.
Workshop duration: 90 minutes

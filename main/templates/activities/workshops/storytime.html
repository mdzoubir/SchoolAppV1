Storytelling workshop in English

What is the Storytime?

In these workshops in English, the actors and actresses who bring the characters to life use their expressiveness to capture the attention of the children, encouraging them to participate and get involved.

What does this workshop consist of?

In the Storytimes we have actors and actresses who enact stories created by the Kids&Us content creators. The stories are designed so that children can enjoy a show in English.

Age: this workshop is aimed at babies ages 1 to 2, as well as children ages 3 to 8. Calendar: the storytelling workshops are carried out in collaboration with organisations such as libraries, civic centres, and bookshops. Contact your nearest school to find out where the workshop is held. Workshop duration: 30 minutes
